W - move camera forward
A - move camera to the left
S - move camera to the right
D - move camera backward

Z - Turn the device on/off
X - Enable bluetooth (shows arrow)
P - Shows road and car example

T - Moves arrow up
F - Moves arrow to the left
H - Moves arrow to the right
G - Moves arrow down

U - Rotate on X-axis positively
J - Rotate on X-axis negatively

I - Rotate on Y-axis positively
K - Rotate on Y-axis negatively

O - Rotate on Z-axis positively
L - Rotate on Z-axis negatively

C - Reset arrow to default position
V - Reset arrow to default rotation
